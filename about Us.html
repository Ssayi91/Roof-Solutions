Our Story

Tonniper Tech Kenya was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable, and reliable roofing and construction materials to builders, contractors, and homeowners across Kenya.

What started as a small supplier of Mabati sheets in Nairobi has grown into a comprehensive solutions provider, offering everything from LGS trusses and uPVC gutters to Decra tiles and wood grain flooring.

Today, we serve clients in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and beyond — always with the same commitment to quality, integrity, and customer empowerment.
