Frequently Asked Questions

Which Mabati sheet is best for a home?
PWF 0.45mm offers the best balance of durability (25–30 years) and cost. Safal is premium for coastal areas.
Do you supply rubber washers with nails?
Yes! We recommend using rubber washers with every nail to prevent leaks. Sold in packs of 100.
How long does Decra tile last?
50+ years with minimal maintenance. It’s stone-coated steel — resistant to wind, fire, and rust.
Are wood grain tiles waterproof?
Yes! They’re made of high-density PVC with a wood finish — ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and wet areas.
Do you supply installation accessories?
Yes — brackets, clips, adhesives, and leveling tools for both roofing and flooring products.
Do you deliver to Utawala?
Yes! Same-day delivery in Nairobi (including Utawala) for orders placed before 2 PM.
What are your payment options?
M-Pesa, bank transfer, or cash on delivery. We also offer 50% upfront for large orders.
Can I visit your factory?
Absolutely! We’re located in the Nairobi Industrial Area. Call ahead for a guided tour.
